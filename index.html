Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

.Usually, we deliver a Professional Landing Page or Starter website within 3 to 7 days. For more complex projects like E-commerce or Multi-page Business websites, it typically takes 10 to 20 days depending on the requirements.

Will my website be mobile responsive?

Yes, 100%. Every website we build is fully responsive, meaning it will look and function perfectly on all devices, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers.

Are there any hidden charges in your pricing plans?

No, we believe in complete transparency. All costs are discussed upfront. The only recurring costs you will have are the annual renewals for your domain and hosting, which are standard across the industry.

Can you integrate online payment gateways?

Absolutely! We can integrate secure payment gateways like Razorpay, PhonePe etc or PayPal, allowing your customers to pay you via UPI, Credit/Debit cards, and Net Banking directly through your website..

Will my website show up on Google searches (SEO)?

Yes. We follow SEO best practices while coding your website. We ensure proper meta tags, fast loading speeds, and search-engine-friendly structures to help your business rank higher on Google.

Do I need to provide the content and images?

If you have your own logo, text, and images, that’s great! If not, don't worry—our team can help you with content writing and selecting professional stock images that match your brand identity.

What kind of support do you provide after the site is live?

We provide dedicated post-launch support. Depending on your plan, we offer maintenance services that include fixing any bugs, updating content, and ensuring your website remains secure and fast.

Can I update the website myself in the future?

Yes. If we build your site using a CMS like WordPress, php HTML or provide an Admin Panel, you can easily update text and images yourself. For custom-coded sites, we provide a user-friendly way or offer maintenance packages to do it for you.

Why are your prices so affordable compared to other agencies?

Our mission is to empower small and medium businesses. By optimizing our development process and focusing on high-performance coding, we are able to provide premium, high-end websites at a price that makes sense for growing businesses.

How do we get started?

It’s simple! You can click the "Free Quote" or "WhatsApp" button on our site. Once we discuss your requirements and receive the initial advance, our team starts the design and development process immediately.

  • Revisions: We provide up to 3 rounds of minor revisions. Major structural changes after approval will incur additional costs.
